Hi! I am a Ph.D student in Robot Learning Laboratory (RLLAB) at Seoul National University advised by Songhwai Oh. Before the Ph.D course, I received a B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from SNU in 2019.
My research interests are lies in developing efficient reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms that can reduce the human efforts for training. Much of my work is about investigating advanced representation learning for scalable & unsupervsed RL.

Research Keywords: Reinforcement Learning, Representation Learning, Planning, Robot Learning
